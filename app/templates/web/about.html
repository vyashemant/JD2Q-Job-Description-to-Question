About JD2Q

The bridge between raw job descriptions and professional interview readiness.

The Mission

JD2Q is a high-precision intelligence tool engineered to deconstruct job descriptions and synthesize expert-level interview architectures. By leveraging state-of-the-art Large Language Models, we enable hiring teams and candidates to align on core competencies with surgical accuracy.

Core Capabilities

  • Semantic Extraction: Analyze JDs for hidden skill requirements.

  • No-Repeat Logic: Regenerate sets without duplicate fatigue.

  • Gold Signals: Generate model answers for every question.

  • Vault Access: Securely manage and rotate multiple API units.

Who We Serve

  • Talent Acquisition: Build structured screening scripts instantly.

  • Engineering Managers: Align technical depth with role seniority.

  • Elite Candidates: Reverse-engineer JDs to master the interview.

Security & Stack

Protocol

Built on a fortified stack of Flask, Supabase (PostgreSQL), and Gemini Pro. All sensitive data traverses via TLS 1.3 and is stored using AES-256 standard encryption.

Ethics

We prioritize data sovereignty. Your keys never touch plaintext logs, and your history is strictly isolated within your authenticated environment.
